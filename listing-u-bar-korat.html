📍 885/8-9 Changphuak Rd, Tambon Nai Mueang, Amphoe Mueang Nakhon Ratchasima, Chang Wat Nakhon Ratchasima 30000, Thailand · ★★★★☆ 4.0 (240 reviews)
About U Bar Korat

U-Bar holds the distinction of being Korat's most renowned and long-standing nightclub. Situated at the junction of Changphuak Road and the Main Road, it follows the typical Thai nightlife scene where local patrons gather in groups to enjoy the ambience. At these tables, it's common to find whiskey bottles being uncorked.

The venue offers a blend of live music and DJ performances during intervals. U-Bar has gained recognition for attracting some of the most alluring girls, making it a preferred spot for the Hi-Sos to revel in.

If this establishment sounds familiar, it could be because they've expanded their reach with sister clubs in Khon Kaen and Ubon Ratchathani, both of which share similar popularity.
