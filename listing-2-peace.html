About 2 Peace

Established in 2008, 2 Peace (a play on "2 pieces," like a bikini) has become one of the thriving after-hours spots in Korat, sharing company with bars like "Ta Wan Deang." It falls under the category of a COYOTE BAR, where ladies perform alluring dances on stage while donning seductive attire.
