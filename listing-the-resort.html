About The Resort

The Resort Entertainment stands as the sole soapy massage parlor in Samui. However, it differs from others in that it lacks a fishbowl setup. Instead, you'll be guided by the mamasan into a spacious room adorned with comfortable couches.

Take a seat beside her as she presents you with the various options available.It's important to note that these prices cater to Farang customers, but through polite negotiation, it may be possible to obtain a slight reduction.

Once you've reached an agreement with the mamasan regarding your desired service, she will summon the available girls to line up in front of you.

Take your time to select your preferred companion before following the mamasan to the designated room, where you can indulge in bliss for the next 90 minutes.
