About Crazy House

Located at the entrance to Soi Cowboy in Sukhumvit Soi 23 Bangkok is the Crazy House Go-Go Bar. The first thing that you notice when you walk in is this place is packed! Okay that wasn't the first thing but I will come to that.

Crazy House is a medium sized Go-Go Bar but has a ground floor and an upper floor therefore is big if put together, on the ground floor there is a peanut shaped elevated dance floor that hold about 16 dancers comfortably.
