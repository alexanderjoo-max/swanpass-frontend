★★★★★
Supposedly, Fortune Spa is a one-stop entertainment, but the disco/bar is always empty. The massage parlour is quite popular though, being one of the only high-class massage parlours in Central Jakarta. My Place and Delta Spa being some other options, though not really considered as upmarket massage joints.

Most of the clients are expats/executives working in Jakarta's business district. It is not a sexual massage parlour with girls and prostitutes like you will find in North Jakarta's red light district (Malioboro, Alexis, V2, Emporium) but a rather mild one with happy ending after the massage (Panti Pijat plus plus).

It is owned by the Comfort Group, which has several massage outlets in Jakarta, among which Comfort Spa, Grand Comfort and Comfort II Batu Tulis.

Facilities of Fortune Spa include: Bar, whirlpool, sauna, fitness center, swimming pool, billiard, mini-theater, shiatsu.

Update June 09: Someone sent a comment saying they had very private VVIP rooms with extra special services provided... but you need to ask the correct person.

Since it is also a hotel, Fortune Spa is open during Ramadhan.
