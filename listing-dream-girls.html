About Dream Girls

On Soi Mango.Exceptionally attractive girls who outshine those in typical beer bars, captivating the audience as they dance on a rotating stage. The unique feature of this venue is that regardless of where you're seated, you have an up-close view of all the dancers, from the front to the back.

Several dancers grace the stage, making eye contact with customers, while others move around, introducing themselves and politely requesting a lady drink (priced at 190 Baht) while joining you at your side.

The atmosphere in the bar is generally relaxed and laid-back.Drink prices at Dream Girls are from 130 Baht for beers to 170-190 Baht for spirits.

The bar fine is 800 Baht before midnight and 600 Baht after, excluding the negotiated price with the dancer for sexual services, which typically starts at 2,000 Baht or more.Based on our conversations with the ladies, it appears that the minimum price for a short time encounter is 1500 baht, while long time engagements range from 2000 to 3000 baht, depending on the lady. These rates might seem steep, especially when considering the availability of more affordable options near Lamai and the Reggae Pub, where some individuals are willing to engage in long time activities for 1000 baht.
