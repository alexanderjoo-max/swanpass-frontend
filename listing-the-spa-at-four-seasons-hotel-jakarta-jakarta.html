About The Spa at Four Seasons Hotel Jakarta

Within the prestigious Capital Place on Jalan Gatot Subroto, The Spa at Four Seasons Hotel Jakarta offers a luxurious retreat from the city's vibrant energy. This urban sanctuary is designed for relaxation and renewal, featuring state-of-the-art facilities including high-tech healing tubs and futuristic "four-senses" loungers.

The spa menu combines ancient Indonesian rituals with modern wellness science. Signature treatments include the Luxury of Lulur, a royal Javanese exfoliation ritual using white turmeric and lemongrass, and the Menard Shiatsu Facial, which blends Eastern techniques with Japanese skincare innovation. Guests can also indulge in the "Double Indulgence" couple’s package or visit the onsite Mirror Mirror Hair Salon and Marco’s Barbershop for complete grooming services.

Location: Four Seasons Hotel Jakarta, Capital Place, Jl. Gatot Subroto Kav. 18.

Specialty: Javanese Lulur, Menard Shiatsu Facials, Couple’s Rituals.

Facilities: Steam rooms, relaxation lounges, salon, and barbershop.

Opening Hours: Daily, 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM.
