About Massage Hoang Long 8

Hoang Long 8 emerged as a fresh addition to the spa landscape in April 2022. Recently brought to our attention by the new management, the establishment boasts a selection of stunning staff members, comparable to those found in KTV lounges. The quality on offer is a rare find, reminiscent only of the high standards seen at Linh Cherry. Despite its slightly remote location, a visit comes highly recommended.

To ensure the best experience, consider visiting during the daytime, before 7 PM, as the selection of available staff tends to diminish later in the night. For those who prefer to pre-select, reaching out to Manager Mr. Quang for the lineup of available staff is an option, or you could make your choice upon arrival.

*Note that the all-inclusive price, which covers tips, is double the rate listed on the menu.

*Personal Review by the Founder:

My recent trip to Hoang Long was notable; the rooms were impressively spacious, equipped with a sauna, shower, inflatable bed, and jacuzzi, alongside a TV and an innovative light system that syncs to the music.

The establishment offers an unconventional but logical sequence of services—starting with a shot followed by the massage, allowing you to relax deeply throughout the experience. The staff's skills reflect rigorous training, indicative of the spa's commitment to quality.

Villa Review (October 2023): The villa, designed for groups of four, offers an unforgettable experience. It starts with beer and soft drinks in the karaoke room, progresses to a pool party, and culminates with massages in private rooms across the upper floors. The provision of laughing gas at 150k per balloon adds to the unique offerings.

For bookings, contact Manager Jonny (Wechat: Quang2417).
