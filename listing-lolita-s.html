About Lolita's

Lolita's is one of the most established and renowned blowjob bars in Bangkok, with quite a history. Located right on Sukhumvit Soi 8, it's conveniently close to the Nana BTS station, attracting a steady stream of both expats and tourists.The bar employs a considerable number of women, usually having around a dozen or more present at any given time.

These ladies sport polo shirts and plaid skirts that give off a school uniform vibe, possibly inspiring the name "Lolita's." Most of the working women here are in their late twenties and thirties.The process for customers is straightforward: you simply walk up to the entrance, pick a lady of your liking, or sometimes, they might take the initiative and choose you. Once you've made your selection, you head upstairs to a private area with curtains drawn, where the services that have made this place famous are offered for 800 Baht.As for the drinks, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic options are available, all at reasonable prices. However, it's not the kind of establishment where people tend to linger just for drinks. Still, there are a few guys who do precisely that. As for their reasons, you'd have to inquire with them to find out more about their preferences.
