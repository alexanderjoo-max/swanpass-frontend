About Prince (800) KTV

Let's shine a light on the main event at 800 KTV - the hostesses. They are like something out of a dream. There are about 40 stunning young women, aged 19-27, who are not just visually appealing but also full of energy and playfulness. And they're well-versed in Vietnamese, English, and Chinese, making them impressively multilingual.

800 KTV is open for business from 5 PM to 2 AM, with rooms offered on an hourly basis. The room prices range from 350K to 600K, depending on size. But when you're in the presence of such dazzling hostesses, the size of the room hardly seems to matter.

Here's something to note: fruits are included in the mandatory room costs, but feel free to decline any additional food items like fried rice, sausages, or fries if they're not to your liking.

When it comes to drinks, there's a wide selection available. Beer is priced at 60k per can, with popular brands like Budweiser and Heineken on offer, and there's also a range of whiskey, cognac, and brandy available for 4-6 million VND per bottle. It's the perfect accompaniment to the enjoyable company.

A gentle reminder that all prices are subject to an additional 10% VAT, and after midnight, there is an extra charge of 100k per hour for the rooms. But considering the quality of the experience, it's a small price for an unforgettable evening.

If you're planning a trip to Ho Chi Minh City, 800 KTV is a must-visit. Dial their booking hotline at +84 0282 2290 800 to secure a memorable night.

Enjoy your time partying!
