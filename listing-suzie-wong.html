About Suzie Wong

Since 2000, Suzie Wong & its gorgeous ladies have been the heart of Soi Cowboy, Bangkok.Chill out on the terrace and soak up the Soi Cowboy vibes at Suzie Wong. Trust us, it's one of the sweetest spots in Bangkok to hit up during happy hour.

Grab an ice-cold beer, plant yourself on the terrace, and enjoy the lively street scene while kicking it with the lovely ladies.Now, let's talk about the main event—the sex shows. Suzie Wong is the last bastion of legit sex shows in Soi Cowboy. Yeah, you heard it right.

They've got the world-famous ping pong shows and other mind-blowing performances every damn night from 10 pm till the early hours. The best part? No extra charge, mate! These shows are seriously top-notch. If you're down to witness some wild action, Suzie Wong is where it's at.Alright, let's dish about the girls.

Look, they might not have the same level of drop-dead gorgeousness as Baccara or Dollhouse (those places are just a hop, skip, and a jump away), but Suzie Wong is upping its game. They've got some stunning, tall, and slim Thai beauties that'll make your head spin.

Get there early, though, if you wanna catch sight of the cream of the crop.Here's the scoop on safety: Suzie Wong has been holding it down for ages and is one of the most legit and secure bars on the block.

The staff is top-notch, the service is on point, and they're all about making sure everyone—guys, gals, and couples—feels welcome while exploring the red-light scene in Bangkok. You can kick back and enjoy the night without a worry.Now, let's talk about bang for your buck.

Suzie Wong might not be the cheapest joint in Soi Cowboy, but it damn sure gives you the best bang for your baht. Sure, drinks might set you back a bit more than other bars (around 180 baht for a beer), but you're getting a killer deal here.
