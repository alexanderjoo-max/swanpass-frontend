About Pannarai

There are a couple of well-kept secrets known to only a select few individuals, and they involve two massage parlors tucked away on a peaceful street near the airport.At Pannarai Massage, you can experience authentic Japanese-style massages using nuru gel, a special substance that intensifies body heat and quickly heightens sensations.

It's important to exercise caution not to reach climax too soon due to its stimulating effects.The Thai massage therapists at this establishment don attractive red Japanese dresses, and upon entering, one of them will accompany you on the couch and provide a menu of services.You can choose from options like the standard nuru gel massage, priced at 1,650 Baht (with a hand job and no removal of clothing), or opt for the full program, which includes a body-to-body treatment and costs 2,950 Baht. Additionally, if you're interested in a prostate massage, it can be arranged for an extra fee of 700 Baht.
