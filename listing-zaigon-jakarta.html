About Zaigon

Zaigon SPA, Lounge and Cafe is one of the best entertainment venues in West Jakarta that you may have never visited. At first glance, this place just looks like a quiet cafe and is suitable for hanging out while enjoying a drink. The location is strategic, the Taman Sari complex is busy which makes the place even more cozy. Are you interested in coming here?

No different from other SPA places, Zaigon offers a calm place that offers a quiet massage room, and a relaxing place to have a drink. This SPA place offers a variety of massage services plus relaxation facilities such as a whirlpool and sauna. SPA places always have their own characteristics and superior services like Zaigon which makes this place busy with visitors every day.
