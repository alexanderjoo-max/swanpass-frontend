About Nuat Kao

Situated within Kosa Hotel, Nuat Kao stands as another establishment offering soapy massage services. Upon entering the hotel's lobby, proceed through the first set of left doors.

The reception staff is known for their welcoming demeanor, and they can assist you if you encounter any difficulty locating the parlor.Nuat Kao presents a selection of appealing attendants who provide soapy massage sessions. The pricing spans from 2,500 to 3,000 baht for a 1.5-hour session, encompassing all services.

Alternatively, they offer a traditional Thai massage priced at 450 baht and an oil massage priced at 600 baht.Engaging with the attendants is a seamless experience, and you'll find them to be quite accommodating.

Arranging for additional services with one of the attendants should be straightforward and hassle-free.
