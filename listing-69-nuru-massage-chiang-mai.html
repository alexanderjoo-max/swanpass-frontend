About 69 Nuru Massage

Welcome to 69 Nuru Massage, a newly opened body-to-body massage parlor in Chiang Mai (March 2025). Nestled in a fully refurbished building adorned with glittering lights, flowing water features, and cozy dimly lit spaces, this establishment offers a unique ambiance that reflects its mainland Chinese roots.Upon arrival, you'll be greeted with a complimentary welcome drink and presented with a menu available in Chinese, English, and Thai. The pricing is accessible, starting at THB 2,200 for a 40-minute session, with premium options like the signature nuru body-to-body massage priced higher. While the nuru massage promises an indulgent experience, some may wonder if the extended session truly delivers on its value.The parlor typically has 5-7 girls on duty, ranging from cute to pretty. It's recommended to request to see the girls in person rather than relying solely on photos, as appearances may differ. Conveniently located off the main road, the venue is easy to find with proper directions. However, it's advisable to avoid arriving via tuk-tuk as additional fees may apply.The name "69 Nuru" adds intrigue but might not fully align with expectations regarding services. Whether you're looking for relaxation or something more adventurous, 69 Nuru Massage aims to provide a memorable experience in Chiang Mai.
