About Angela Massage

Angela Massage Jakarta is a specialized outcall massage service bringing professional relaxation directly to your hotel, apartment, or home in Jakarta. Designed for convenience and comfort, they operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ensuring you can unwind whenever you need it.

The service features a team of young, friendly, and well-trained female therapists (including staff like Ms. Shasa, Ms. Lili, and Ms. Beby) who are ready to provide a refreshing full-body massage. They are known for their "open-minded" and polite attitude, aiming to relieve stress and rejuvenate your body and mind. Pricing is transparent, typically around IDR 500,000 for a 90-minute session (inclusive of transport in Central Jakarta), making it a competitive choice for travelers and residents alike.
