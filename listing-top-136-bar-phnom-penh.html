About Top 136 Bar

Top 136: A Unique Chinese-Style Hostess Bar

Phnom Penh's hostess bar scene has seen a surge in recent years, with a variety of establishments catering to diverse tastes. Among these, Top 136 stands out as a Chinese-inspired venue offering a distinct experience.

Located near Ostro Bistro, Top 136 boasts a stylish interior reminiscent of a high-end Asian karaoke club. Its plush seating and attentive table service create a luxurious ambiance, setting it apart from the more traditional bars in the area.

While open to all, Top 136's aesthetic and clientele lean towards a Chinese influence. The bar's primary draw is its attractive staff, known for their beauty and sophistication. Prices are slightly higher than average, reflecting the premium experience.

It's worth noting that many women at Top 136 may not be inclined to leave with customers, as the bar itself is a popular destination for socializing and enjoying drinks. While Westerners are welcome, the venue's appeal primarily lies within the Asian community.

Instead of the normal bar setup, it's got comfy couches and stuff. It's basically a lounge, but with a lot of attractive ladies. The waiters bring your drinks straight to your seat, so it's like table service, but it's included, not extra.

The main draw here is obviously the girls. They're pretty and well-built, and they're always changing, so there's always someone new to check out. The owners also have another place nearby, and they sometimes move girls between the two. It's kind of like those places you see in Asian movies.

The drinks are a bit pricier than normal, and the ladies are also asking for more money to leave with you. But hey, you're getting some pretty high-quality company.

I don't think many girls actually leave with customers every night. It seems more like a place to drink and hang out with friends. A lot of Chinese and Asian guys come here, but not as many Westerners.

Overall, Top 136 is a bit different from other bars in the area. It's more like a lounge, it's a bit pricier, but it's got some really attractive girls. Whether it's worth it or not depends on what you're looking for. I'd give it three stars.
