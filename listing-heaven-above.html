About Heaven Above

Heaven Above has been a local institution in Pattaya since 2004.Originally we were in Soi Diamond & upstairs from Super Baby but since covid we were forced to move our popular club to a new location in Soi Boomerang ( which runs off Soi Buakhow )We have moved away from our old Go Go concept & have become a Gentlemans Club with a more “hands on approach”Our hospitality & ability to take care of our customers is never in question & our staff ( of course our gorgeous ladies ) are 2nd to none in Pattaya.Please feel free to join us when you next visit Pattaya as we are sure the experience will leave you wanting more.
