About Suzy Wong's 3

If you are in Patong for a serious party, aim for Bangla Walking Street and, more precisely Soi Seadragon.

It’s easy to find us with our new red club front with two ancient terra cotta warriors on each side of the main door.Come in! Don’t be shy! The entrance is free, and our drinks start at a surprisingly low price, less than a hundred baht for a local beer!Once inside, you will not regret it.

We are proud of our new decor; we worked hard to make it look stylish and perfect. We added a superb bar, great lighting and sound system, plus a talented DJ to create the right atmosphere to make this night unforgettable.The most beautiful girls dance on selected tracks, and your jaw will undoubtedly drop.

There are no other clubs in Phuket like Suzy Wong’s 3. You know what we mean if you have been to our legendary and original Suzy Wong’s.

With Suzy Wong’s One, we set the standards for the party in Phuket, bringing it to a brand new level at Suzy Wong’s 3.The fun doesn’t stop here! At midnight, the wall separating Suzy Wong’s 3 to our other famous ‘Harem Club‘ opens up, and the fun just grows double as you can enjoy both clubs at the same time!Entrance is totally FREE, and beers start at 79 Baht! Your bill ALWAYS comes with your drink, no overcharge, no rip off and no bad surprise. We make sure of that, and we are proud of it!
