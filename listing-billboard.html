About Billboard

***Yeah, yeah — Billboard is a Go-go, but it’s the best in Bangkok so we’ve included it on Swanpass. High prices, but the girls are OUTSTANDINGBillboard first opened its doors on the top floor of Nana Plaza in 2011, replacing Hollywood Carousel.

The bar raised eyebrows with the installation of its giant Jacuzzi – a first in Bangkok – but never reached its full potential until current management took over in 2015.Today, Billboard Bangkok is Thailand’s No. 1 go-go bar, voted year-after-year the best in the country by both guests and nightlife reviewers.

Every night more than 100 of Thailand’s most beautiful women can be found spinning around on the carousel stage or frolicking in the Jacuzzi.

Billboard’s high standards means you’ll find only the top talent the industry has to offer.Combine the incredible lineup of dancers with an unrivaled mix of music, a multi-million-baht sound system and a computer-programmed system with more than 200 than 200 lights and you’ve got a party bar like no other.

Many have said Billboard is more five-star nightclub than go-go bar. Who are we to disagree?Billboard has managed to stay atop the mountain for so long because of the meticulous, hands-on management by Billboard’s owners, who refuse to compromise on quality.

If it’s not the best, then you won’t find it in Billboard.Billboard is open seven nights a week from 8 p.m. until “late”. So when you come to Nana Plaza, head straight to the top floor for the Billboard Experience.
