Day and Night Bar Complex

📍 Sampanthamit Rd muong Amphoe Mueang Udon Thani, Chang Wat Udon Thani 41000, Thailand · ★★★★☆ 3.5 (104 reviews)
About Day and Night Bar Complex

Among the various city locales, the Day and Night establishment stands out as a widely frequented nightlife spot in Udon Thani, particularly for foreign visitors seeking entertainment. Positioned on Soi Samphan Thamit, adjacent to Prajak Siilapacom, it shares proximity with the Nutty Park complex.

Further along, you'll find the night market and railway station.This expansive complex exudes a laid-back ambiance and features amicable hostesses. The spectrum of ages and attractiveness levels among the hostesses is diverse.

However, assessing personal appeal remains a subjective matter, making it impossible to definitively label any as 'striking.' Many possess their own charm in unique ways, yet one common thread binds them all: adeptness in engaging with patrons.

This skill is honed not only here but also in locations like Pattaya, Phuket, Bangkok, and other spots where enthusiastic foreigners seek their company.The bars situated at the complex's entrance exhibit higher patronage compared to those at the opposite end, which often remain deserted.

The latter establishments tend to compensate for the lack of footfall by upping their beer prices, though they don't enjoy the constant stream of visitors like their more central counterparts. Unlike the often aggressive touting seen in Pattaya, Udon Thani differs in its approach—here, bar staff won't forcefully drag you in.

Most will greet you and offer an enticing comment or gesture, but harassment is absent. Taking a preliminary tour of all the bars to assess available options is encouraged and well-received.Day and Night comprises 16 bars, although not all host working girls. The majority, however, feature at least a couple of hopefuls seeking favorable encounters.

The Scotsman, located on the left side, operates as a bar without female companions. Hence, if you desire a tranquil drinking experience minus the attention of Thai hostesses, this establishment provides a haven.
