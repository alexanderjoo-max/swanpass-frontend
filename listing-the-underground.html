★★★★☆
I did not stay at this hotel however....

My wife is from Khon Kaen and so on our visit to her home town she had to come to this hotel to sing some Karaoke, thus I learned about Show Time Karaoke located in the sub level of the hotel next to the snooker hall.
The airconditioned environment was a fantastic reprieve from the heat. Comfortable sofas adorn the main stage area and the atmosphere was quite nice.
Drinks are cold and the food was good but best of all the prices were very reasonable.
There are VIP rooms that seat up to about 15 people where you can sing to your hearts content.
The service is very good and for those single guys there is a choice of over 30 ladies which are more than happy to provide company at your request while you howl out your favorite songs. They do not "hound you" like many places in Thailand, providing more of an upscale feel and atmosphere for people like my wife that just love to sing (as best they can anyway) 😀

All in all the place was nice and the vip rooms run about 300 baht per hour.
The place like most entertainment venues gets crackin' after 11 pm so during our time there, it was just getting busy near our departure time.

A little place to check out that is NOT on Google maps.
