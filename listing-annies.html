About Annie's

Opened in 1972 The World-Famous Annie’s was the longest running business of its type in the Nana/Sukhumvit area, if not Bangkok, which closed its doors 2014. Annie’s kept its email address open to talk to its old customer and keep in contact with old friends. Covid come and went and changed the nightlife landscape for good.

One of Annie’s past business partners that had their own site came to Annie and said “I have a site that we cannot keep open no more, it is a great one but we need to focus on our others at the moment” Annie said “I will take it over to help you as long as it has my name on it and my way of doing business” a deal was struck and the world-famous Annie’s is open again but on soi 7/1.With customers from all over the world including expatriates, Annie’s special service is renowned for its “well you all know what” This is Bangkok is it not?When visiting Annie’s, you can be sure of Customer Service with a Personalised feel, Choice of staff, open attitude, competitive prices, fully stocked bar, and honest recommendations about our staff and much more. Annie’s when it opened in 1972 was the only business of this type in Bangkok focused solely non indigenous clients 1 2 3Since then and like all businesses, Annie’s has gone through its ups and downs but since making it even after its short closure one of the key nightlife talking points of Bangkok. On many internets polls Annie’s was voted Thailand’s Best. Now we have reopened our with modern new rooms 2 different styles we are ready to make our mark again. Annie’s due to its past popularity has a long hard road to get it past position back and besides by doing what we did before we have one more special thing to announce, the original Annie herself will be running our new site.
