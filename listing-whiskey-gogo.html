About Whiskey & GoGO

Whiskey & Go Go can be found on the second floor of Nana Plaza. As you enter, you'll notice the name of the bar illuminated in neon right above the door.

Speaking of the door, it's an intricate antique wooden piece that stands out among any other venue of its kind worldwide.The gals who've got more experience seem pretty stoked to work at Whiskey & Go Go. I've actually spotted some familiar faces from other bars here, so it seems like they've managed to snag some top-notch performers from elsewhere.

I reckon the fact that they don't have some strict mamasan breathing down their necks might have something to do with it.
