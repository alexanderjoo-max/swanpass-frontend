About Suzy Wong's

Phuket’s famous Suzy Wong is a group of bars, gentlemen’s clubs, strip clubs, lounges and go-go bars all located on the popular Bangla Walking Street in Patong Beach, on the west coast of Phuket Island.With its unmissable red Chinese façade, Suzy Wong’s is the most famous and, without a doubt, the best strip club in Phuket.

Suzy Wong’s always features the most beautiful and sexy Thai girls in Phuket, offering great service and fun company. With great music, affordable drinks and free entry, it has become an icon of Phuket nightlife.

For more than two decades, Suzy has been on the must-see list of many travellers as the most unforgettable way to end the day with a blast!But don’t stop the party at the first club! Check out our many other bars and lounges, each of them with its theme, design and girls’ costumes: Suzy Wong’s II (Devils Playground), Suzy Wong’s 3 (our latest and most impressive club!), Harem, Rock Hard’s Crazy Girls. All are much talked about and are recommended by many travellers and locals as the perfect place for a truly unforgettable night of entertainment. You can even book your VIP table in advance or call us directly at 087 272 2588We are well known to be “The Original Ass Smacking Fun Bars” in Phuket, a concept that was often copied by other bars but never equalled, not even close!
