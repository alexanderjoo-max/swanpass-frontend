About Massage Bangkok City

Massage BANGKOK CITY stands at the forefront as the premier venue in the men’s massage circuit, boasting the distinction of being the most upscale facility currently in Ho Chi Minh City. It's often regarded as the pinnacle of the service industry.

Situated at 118-120 Yen The, a stone’s throw from Tan Son Nhat airport, BANGKOK CITY serves as a prime stop for both local and international patrons. The establishment merges the charm of Asian aesthetics with the opulence of modern European design in its room offerings. It's a must-visit location. Notably, there is the convenience of selecting your masseuse through an iPad.

Refreshment options include a selection of soft drinks, beer, and noodles.

The prices are comprehensive; however, it's advisable to confirm with the establishment regarding whether gratuities are included in the room cost or if they're separate.

For bookings, contact the hotline at 0938-779-777.
