About Suzy Wong's 2 - Devil's Playground

Devil’s Playground (also known as Suzy Wong’s II) is the naughtiest A Go-Go bar in Patong and one of the nicest.

Located in Soi Sea Dragon just off Soi Bangla, you will instantly recognise the devil’s cave entrance guarded by some very hot devil girls.Don’t be shy and step in; like most Go-Go bars in Phuket, drink prices are fixed and written on the menu, and the bill will always be brought to you with your drinks.

If you are still hesitating, you can always have a sneak peek inside and decide if this will fit your party for tonight… if you choose to check other bars, no one with harass you.Entrance is totally FREE, and beers start at 79 Baht! Your bill ALWAYS comes with your drink, no overcharge, no rip off and no bad surprise.

We make sure of that, and we are proud of it!
