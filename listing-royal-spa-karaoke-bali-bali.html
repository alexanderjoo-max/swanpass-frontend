About ROYAL SPA & KARAOKE BALI

Located on Jalan Gunung Soputan in Denpasar, Royal Spa & Karaoke Bali (RPS Spa Plus) stands out as a premier one-stop destination for relaxation and entertainment. Unlike unparalleled traditional wellness centers, this facility uniquely combines a luxury spa experience with a full-service karaoke lounge, catering to those looking to unwind in style.

The venue is well-regarded for its modern, spacious rooms and cleanliness. Guests can enjoy a variety of "opulent treatments," including Traditional Balinese massage and specialized therapeutic packages designed to relieve stress. Whether visiting for a private massage session or hosting a social gathering in the karaoke rooms, the establishment offers professional service with a focus on guest satisfaction.

Packages typically range from Standard to VIP, making it a popular choice for the executive lifestyle crowd in Bali.

Open daily from 12:00 PM to 12:00 AM
