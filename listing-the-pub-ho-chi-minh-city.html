About The Pub

The local watering hole in Ho Chi Minh City known as The Pub has gained quite a reputation as a typical modern hostess bar in Saigon. While some establishments in the area still feature women awaiting gentlemen to settle off-the-books bar fines, those might capture the attention of a specific audience.

However, in today's scene, most bars are populated by women simply enjoying drinks and socializing with patrons. This fact may defy the expectations of those with a more scandalized perspective or perhaps harboring wishful thoughts, but it is the prevailing reality.
