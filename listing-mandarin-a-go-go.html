About Mandarin a-Go-Go

Directly ahead of you, upon stepping off the escalator, lies Mandarin GoGo Bar—an establishment with ownership connections to Mandarin Table Dance and Shark GoGo Bar in Soi Cowboy. Despite its modest size, Mandarin GoGo Bar features two levels.

While the popular Happy Hour at Mandarin has been discontinued, the bar still showcases a delightful selection of gogo dancers. In fact, the gogo girls at Mandarin are often regarded as more appealing and younger compared to those found in many other gogo bars within Nana Plaza.

It's definitely worth exploring!
