About Ginza Entertainment

The premier soapy massage establishment in Korat takes the lead in every aspect. It excels across the board: the girls are the youngest, possess the fairest complexions, and are the most appealing (none of whom have children—I verified this with the papasan, who informed me that they don't accept girls with children).

Furthermore, it boasts the most inviting and contemporary rooms, complete with mirrors adorning the bed area. Even the lobby itself is a pleasant space to enjoy a couple of beers beforehand.

While the prices here are the highest in the city (although still notably reasonable when compared to Bangkok), they stand at either 2,000 Baht or 2,300 Baht depending on the specific girl.The unique setup includes a long red couch upon which the girls are seated, without any glass partition. This arrangement is set within a jungle-like atmosphere.
