About Brix Bar

BRIX, located in the basement of the Grand Hyatt Singapore, is one of the city's most vibrant nightlife destinations. Known for its upscale ambiance and live music, it attracts a diverse crowd of expats, tourists, and professionals.

A standout feature of BRIX is its reputation for hosting an array of stunning women from around the world, including those from Asia, Eastern Europe, and South America. These women are often described as elegant and well-dressed, contributing to the venue's lively yet sophisticated atmosphere.

While BRIX is known as a hotspot for socializing and meeting new people, it maintains a clean and non-sleazy environment, making it a unique experience for visitors seeking a high-energy night out with excellent music and company
