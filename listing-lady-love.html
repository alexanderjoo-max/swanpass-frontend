Lady Love Club is the ultimate nightspot and gogo club for the discerning gentleman in Pattaya. If you enjoy relaxing with friendly gorgeous girls, then you will discover that our 60 beautiful girls are the best in Pattaya. We’re located in LK Metro, Pattaya’s most vibrant area for fun.

We also offer our nightly happy hour from 8pm-9pm. Sunday night is happy hour prices all night!We can put you in the company of the most beautiful dancers Thailand has to offer, with exotic girls from every corner of the Kingdom. Experience the glitz and glamour whilst Indulging in our premium bottle service with champagne and spirits.Celebrate an event with friends or just go crazy with some friends.
