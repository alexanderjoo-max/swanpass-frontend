About RCA Entertainment

There's just one red light district with girly bars in Ao Nang—contrary to what some nightlife guides might suggest. It goes by the name RCA Entertainment and boasts nearly two dozen bars brimming with girls.

So, if your game plan involves soaking in stunning beaches by day and enjoying the company of equally stunning girls by night, you'll likely end up at RCA each and every evening.
