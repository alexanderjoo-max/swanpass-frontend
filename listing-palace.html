About Palace

Palace Massage finds its home in a sizable edifice along Phalochai Road, easily identifiable at night due to its prominent red exterior lights.

Despite the conspicuously large "PALACE" sign in Thai, the establishment actually maintains a relatively discreet presence.The building itself displays signs of wear, yet it doesn't quite reach the level of dilapidation seen in some other soapy massage parlors across Thailand, where unaddressed water damage has compromised entire structures.

Once inside, a spacious lobby adorned with enticing posters greets you. However, it's worth noting that a significant portion of the space remains unutilized.Ample seating for patrons dots the area, though these seats appear to see little use.

The glass partition and fishbowl area are situated to the left, yet the women on staff do not confine themselves to this space. Instead, the three or four working ladies opt to sit on chairs in front of the glass or linger outside, promptly approaching upon spotting a potential customer.At Palace, two Thai attendants preside over the lobby.

They exude a warm welcome to customers, with at least one of them fluent in English. While this amicable attitude might stand out in places like Bangkok or even Pattaya, it's well in line with the warm and hospitable demeanor found in Isaan.

Here, even foreigners in search of adult entertainment are embraced with open arms due to the region's inherent warmth and openness.
