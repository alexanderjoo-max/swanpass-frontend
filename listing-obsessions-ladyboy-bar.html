About Obsessions Ladyboy Bar

Welcome to Obsession, quite possibly the finest ladyboy bar in Bangkok. This spacious club boasts a classic layout, featuring a large central stage adorned with ample space and dancing poles for numerous stunning and feminine girls. Surprisingly fluent in English and even a bit of Japanese, these ladies exude beauty.

While they may attempt to capture your attention by waving from the stage, they aren't overly assertive. However, it won't be long before they kindly request a lady drink, which, as a courteous gesture, you should consider purchasing...well, sort of.The prices at Obsession are slightly higher than the usual, yet still reasonable.

A beer and a lady drink both come to 150 baht, ensuring your bill remains free of any unpleasant surprises. It's undoubtedly an enjoyable place to visit, but it's worth noting that Obsession may not be the ideal choice for beginners.

During our inspection visit, we had to politely excuse ourselves as the ladies became a bit too friendly for our intended purpose.
