About Chhing’s

Chhing’s Bar is situated on Street 144, close to the corner of Street 19. With its understated black-and-glass exterior, it’s easy to miss unless you know exactly where to look. It sits just across from Lucky Express Market, not far from the bustling riverside, but it isn’t in one of the more bar-dense areas like Streets 104, 130, or 136. Despite the quieter surroundings, a few bars have managed to carve out a space here over the years.

Staff and atmosphere Chhing’s Bar is staffed by just under a dozen women, not counting Chhing herself. For special events or parties, additional staff often seem to appear. The roster of hostesses is constantly evolving, as is common in the industry, with new faces being added regularly.

The women working at Chhing’s are an eclectic bunch, mostly between their mid-20s to early 30s. Some are more experienced than others, but all are comfortable and engaging with foreign patrons. Unlike some bars where staff may seem reserved or unfamiliar with the role, the ladies at Chhing’s know how to make customers feel welcome. Though a couple of the women might not fit the stereotypical petite local figure, they are all well-dressed, confident, and accustomed to the hostess bar scene.

While the vibe at Chhing’s can occasionally get lively—with the odd tipsy hostess dancing on a table—the bar sticks to its core identity as a hostess venue. The main focus is providing a laid-back space for patrons to relax with the company of friendly hostesses. The women make their money through a combination of salary and commissions from lady drinks, and while there is a barfine system in place, it’s not a given that every hostess will leave with a customer. This approach can sometimes be confusing for those more accustomed to the go-go bar or brothel scene, but it’s part of the charm of Phnom Penh’s hostess bars.

Overall experience Hostess bars like Chhing’s offer a unique social experience, and this one is no different. The bar may not be the flashiest, and the staff aren’t bikini-clad models, but it’s a well-run establishment where patrons can enjoy good company and lively conversation. Chhing’s draws on years of experience in the industry, and it shows. If you’re looking for a friendly, casual night out in Phnom Penh with a few drinks and some engaging company, Chhing’s Bar is a solid choice.
