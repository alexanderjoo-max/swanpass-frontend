About Geylang District

The only legal red light district in Singapore! Geylang, Singapore's most infamous red-light district, is located on the eastern fringe of the city. It is renowned for its vibrant nightlife and the presence of over 100 licensed brothels, primarily concentrated along the even-numbered lorongs (lanes) such as Lorong 16, 18, and 20. Despite its seedy reputation, Geylang operates under strict regulations, with brothels required to obtain government licenses and adhere to health and safety standards. Sex workers must undergo regular medical screenings and carry a government-issued yellow card.
