About Du Xuân Hotel & Massage

On a vibrant street lined with shops, this bright high-rise hotel is 4 km from the popular Bến Thành Market and a 2-minute walk from the nearest transit station.

Amenities include a bright, funky karaoke room, a laid-back bar, and a spa with massage services, a sauna and an ornately tiled hot tub.
