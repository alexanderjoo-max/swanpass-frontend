About Christin Massage

Christin Massage is the exclusive body-to-body Thai massage services in Patong.Although there are a few similar massage parlors in Phuket town, they tend to cater more to locals, so tourists often opt for Christin Massage instead.

After all, it can be a bit of a hassle to travel all the way to Phuket town for a massage, especially if you’re staying at one of the many hotels on the island’s west coast.When you arrive at Christin Massage, you’ll be welcomed by the staff and escorted to the first floor.

The manager will ask if you’d like a drink and inquire about the type of massage you’re interested in. Meanwhile, you’ll be able to peruse the selection of girls dressed in attractive clothing and sporting numbers on their attire.Once you’ve made your choice, the manager will inform the girl, and the two of you will head upstairs to a private room.

All of the rooms at Christin Massage are equipped with air conditioning, a TV, a double bed, and a bathtub.

The girl will draw a warm bath for you both, and then proceed to give you an unforgettable Thai oil massage on an inflatable mattress.If you’re a couple, you’ll be pleased to know that Christin Massage welcomes couples and even offers separate or shared rooms for them.

However, it’s worth noting that some guests have found the service a bit impersonal, while others have thoroughly enjoyed it.
