About Starmoon Bar

Located in the Kota Indah complex in Taman Sari, Starmoon Bar is a well-known destination for Jakarta's nightlife scene. Often described as a lively watering hole, it offers a casual yet energetic atmosphere perfect for late-night gatherings. The venue is popular for its straightforward, colorful setting and often features live music or entertainment that keeps the vibe upbeat.

Guests typically visit for the drinks, with a selection of beers, wines, and cocktails available. It serves as a social hub for locals and tourists alike who are looking to unwind in the historic Pinangsia area. While known for its "happy hour" promotions and attentive service compared to similar local bars, it is best suited for those seeking a raw, authentic local nightlife experience rather than a polished luxury lounge.

Location: Komplek Ruko Kota Indah, Pinangsia, West Jakarta.

Vibe: Casual, Lively, Late-Night Entertainment.

Specialty: Cocktails, Beer, Live Entertainment.

Operating Hours: Typically 4:00 PM – 12:00 AM (Verify before visiting).
