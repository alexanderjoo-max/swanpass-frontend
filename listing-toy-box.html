About Toy Box

Toy Box, situated in Soi 6, Pattaya, is an immensely popular short-time bar. It falls under the ownership of the Nightwish group of bars, which operates several establishments along Soi 6. The lineup of girls at Toy Box generally consists of both average-looking ladies and some stunning beauties.
