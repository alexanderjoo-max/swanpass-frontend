About People's Park Centre

People's Park Centre in Singapore is a mixed-use building known for its vibrant shopping and dining options, but it also has a lesser-known aspect related to the sex industry. The centre houses several massage parlours, some of which have been reported to offer illicit services. These establishments often feature provocatively dressed women who openly solicit customers outside the shops. While these activities are not officially part of a designated red-light district like Geylang, they contribute to a shadowy aspect of the area's nightlife. However, it's worth noting that such activities are illegal and subject to enforcement by local authorities. The area remains generally safe, with legitimate businesses operating alongside these clandestine services, though some business owners have expressed concerns about the impact on the mall's reputation.

Reported prices: Massage: $30SGD HJ/FJ: $50-150SGD
