About Orchard Tower

Orchard Towers, located on Orchard Road in Singapore, is an 18-story mixed-use building that gained notoriety as an unofficial red-light district. Often referred to as the "Four Floors of Whores," it was known for its nightlife venues, including bars and clubs, where freelance sex workers from diverse countries such as Thailand, the Philippines, Indonesia, and Eastern Europe operated. Unlike traditional red-light districts like Geylang, Orchard Towers did not house brothels; instead, it served as a meeting place for clients and sex workers who would then proceed to nearby hotels.
