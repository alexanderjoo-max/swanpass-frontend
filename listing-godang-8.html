About Godang 8

Food and beverage outlets with live music, modern style and wide open spaces.In the same neighborhood as the girly bars along Maharaj Road, you'll find the three most popular nightclubs in Krabi Town. Godang 8 (โกดัง 8) offers a blend of a hostess bar and a nightclub vibe.

It's got the typical Thai club setup, with small tables where folks gather in groups, often sharing a bottle of whiskey. The live band adds to the atmosphere by playing Thai hits.What makes Godang 8 stand out is the abundance of young and incredibly attractive Krabi girls working there.

They function as hostesses and are more than happy to join you as long as you're up for sharing some drinks and treating them to a few shots. These shots go for 120 Baht each, and they might suggest a couple in succession. Keep in mind that the Godang 8 girls can't leave with you until the club closes at 2 a.m.

What you choose to do afterward is entirely your call if you decide to hang out longer.
