About Bali Spa Legian

Located centrally on Jalan Padma (right next to the Lokha Hotel), Bali Spa & Wellnesscenter Legian offers a peaceful sanctuary amidst the energy of the Legian district. Known for its "Serenity at Home" concept, the spa provides both in-house treatments and convenient mobile services to nearby villas and hotels.

Guests can choose from a full menu of traditional therapies, including authentic Balinese massage, reflexology, and beauty treatments like manicures and pedicures.

Open daily from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM, the facility is praised for its professional therapists and relaxing atmosphere. Prices are competitive for the area, offering excellent value for travelers seeking high-quality care without the luxury resort price tag.
