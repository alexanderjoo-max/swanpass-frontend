About Thermae (Ruamchit Hotel)

One of the best places to find freelancers in Bangkok!Have you heard about Thermae Cafe in Bangkok? If not, let us fill you in on this basement gem at Ruamchitt Plaza Hotel. During my time in Thailand, folks often ask about the bars that have stood the test of time, and one name that comes up is Thermae.

Despite the changes, including prices, this iconic Bangkok spot is still going strong.This unique bar, also known as the 'Star Wars' bar, is a fascinating place where you'll encounter travelers of all sorts with diverse backgrounds.

It's an intriguing visit, even if you're not planning to hang out with the girls.What sets Thermae apart is that the girls here work independently, not employed by the bar. This bar dates back to the '60s, a favorite haunt for soldiers during the Vietnam War, seeking relaxation and a chance to meet local Thai women.

Back then, things were way more affordable. It soon became a popular spot for expats and single male foreigners and gained even more popularity over the years.With the advent of the internet and online forums, more guys discovered this place and its allure. Many visit Bangkok for the red light districts, and Thermae remains a top choice.

Plus, it's more budget-friendly compared to go-go bars. If you prefer to meet women outside the bar scene, there's a thriving online community for that too.Thermae sits on Sukhumvit Road, one of Bangkok's most famous tourist streets, in the basement of Ruamchitt Plaza Hotel.

It's well-loved by travelers, and many end up taking a freelancer from Thermae to the hotel's upstairs rooms for some fun. If you're not a fan of this hotel, Sofitel Bangkok next door is an alternative. The bar has a relaxed, easygoing vibe where you can chill at the bar or a table.
