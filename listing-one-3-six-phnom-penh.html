About One 3 Six

A Casual Take on One 3 SixOne 3 Six: A Phnom Penh Staple

One 3 Six, or 136 Bar, is a classic Phnom Penh hostess bar that's been around for ages. It's not your typical old-school dive, but it's got that timeless charm. Think of it as the neighborhood bar you always come back to.

What Sets It Apart?

Live Music: Catch a live show three nights a week.

Good Drinks: The bartenders know their stuff.

Friendly Staff: The mamasan and the ladies are always welcoming.

Great Location: It's right in the heart of the action.

Why You Should Check It Out

If you're looking for a place to grab a drink, meet some locals, and enjoy a bit of entertainment, One 3 Six is a solid choice. It's not the flashiest bar in town, but it's got a laid-back vibe that's hard to beat.
