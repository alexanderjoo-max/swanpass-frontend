About Sumo Spa and Shiatsu

Located in the Kokan Pertama complex on Jl. Boulevard Bukit Gading Raya, Sumo Shiatsu & Spa is a long-standing wellness destination in North Jakarta. This facility combines traditional relaxation with modern entertainment, offering a wide range of amenities including a sauna, whirlpool, steam room, and a fully equipped gym.

Guests can indulge in professional treatments such as Shiatsu, Traditional Massage, and Reflexology. Beyond therapeutic services, the venue features a lounge, restaurant, and bar, making it a popular spot for socializing and unwinding after hours. With private rooms and VIP options available, it caters to those seeking both privacy and a comprehensive leisure experience.
