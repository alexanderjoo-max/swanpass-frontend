About Pink Panther

Best bar in Bangkok since the 1980sSince its inception in the 1980s, Pink Panther has consistently been a shining beacon in the midst of Bangkok’s vibrant Patpong Soi 2 nightlife.

This revered gogo bar seamlessly combines affordability, charm, and thrilling entertainment, marking it as an unbeatable destination in the city’s after-hours scene.For decades, patrons have been drawn to its unique blend of experiences and its reputation as a scam-free, honest establishment.

Open from 8 PM until late, Pink Panther promises an unforgettable evening.Over 50 delightful ladies, each dedicated to ensuring patrons have a memorable night, add to the pulsating rhythm of the nightlife.

And as the clock strikes 11 PM, an electrifying, free live boxing show provides an adrenaline-infused spectacle that’s as thrilling as it is unique.However, what truly distinguishes Pink Panther is its commitment to value.

Having one of the most competitively priced drink menus among Bangkok’s gogo bars, it allows patrons to savor everything from classic cocktails to fine wines and a variety of beers, all without burning a hole in their pockets.

Anchored in integrity and committed to offering a scam-free environment, Pink Panther has been a reliable part of Bangkok’s nightlife since the 1980s.It’s more than just a bar – it’s a legacy, a guarantee of a fun-filled, affordable, and trustworthy night out in the heart of Bangkok.
