About Morena Pub & KTV

Morena Pub & KTV is a well-known nightlife destination in Batam, located in the Windsor Square complex in Nagoya. It functions as a hybrid entertainment venue combining a lively pub atmosphere with private KTV (karaoke) rooms.

The venue is popular for its energetic vibe, featuring resident DJs, pool tables, and dance performances. It is often recognized as a social spot for those seeking "companionship," with hostess services available, particularly in the VIP karaoke rooms. The location is convenient, situated next to the popular Windsor Food Court, making it a common stop for late-night entertainment in the city.
