About Lamaroca Spa

Lamaroca Spa is a premier "Spa Leisure" destination located in the upscale Wijaya Grand Centre, South Jakarta. Part of the renowned Deep Spa Group, this sanctuary is designed to offer more than just relaxation; it provides an immersive sensory experience that rejuvenates the body, mind, and soul. The spa is famous for its intimate ambiance and high-end facilities, including Luxury Rooms with private jacuzzis, The Suite for couples, and the ultra-exclusive Mansion room.

Guests can indulge in signature therapies such as the "Supreme" (2 therapists) and "Grandeur" (3 therapists) body-to-body massages, specifically crafted to heighten sensory pleasure. With a sophisticated lounge bar and a focus on privacy and opulence, Lamaroca Spa is the ultimate urban escape for those seeking a transformative and intimate wellness journey. Location: Wijaya Grand Centre, Blok F No. 35-36, Kebayoran Baru, Jakarta Selatan. Specialty: Sensory Body-to-Body Massage, Couple Spa, Multi-Therapist Treatments. Facilities: Private Jacuzzi Suites, Lounge & Bar, VIP Rooms. Operating Hours: Daily, 11:30 AM – 11:30 PM.
