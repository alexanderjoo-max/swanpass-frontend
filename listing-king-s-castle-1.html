About King's Castle 1

You know what's interesting? The King's Castle go-go bar used to be a piece of cake to find. But nowadays, things have changed big time. Patpong isn't as hot as it once was. The whole place looks kinda dark and even a bit lonesome. Sure, there are some folks wandering the streets, but the crazy crowds that used to pack this alley are long gone.

The adult businesses still hanging around probably hate this new normal, but hey, it does make it easier to get around. Sometimes, you can still spot a few people hanging out in the area at certain times. Quite a shift, huh?
