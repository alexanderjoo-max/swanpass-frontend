About Butterflies

With 140 ladies, two stages and an oversized Jacuzzi, Butterflies is Nana Plaza Bangkok‘s largest and most exciting go-go bar.Butterflies Bangkok, located on the same floor as the renowned Billboard agogo in Nana Plaza, is owned and operated by the same team.

This connection holds significance because Billboard is widely recognized as one of the best gogo bars in Bangkok, celebrated for its sensational shows, exceptional service, and, of course, stunning girls.

The management has applied their tried-and-true formula for selecting exceptional girls to Butterflies as well.When it comes to the girls at Butterflies Bangkok, you'll be spoiled for choice.

With approximately 50 to 80 girls on weeknights and up to 100 on weekends, all young, fit, and just the right amount of wild, there's a girl to suit every taste.Butterflies sets itself apart from other bars in the building by offering a welcoming and distinct atmosphere.

As soon as you step inside, you'll be greeted by two stages flanking the bar, each adorned with poles for the girls to showcase their dance moves. But what truly sets Butterflies apart is the large Jacuzzi positioned in the center of the bar, which serves as the stage for...The sexy foam shows that unfold at Butterflies.

Prepare to be captivated as the girls revel in the water, lather themselves in foam, and indulge in sensuous interactions.
