About Soi 80

Hua Hin's second red-light district with go-go bars and freelancers.Soi 80 was once the preferred choice among expats for Hua Hin's happy ending massage district. However, it maintains a low-key vibe, and the girls on this street are by no means assertive.

Nowadays, you'll discover a plethora of massage services near the junction of Poon Suk Road and Soi 57 (approximately 100 meters north of Soi Bintabaht).

Still, Soi 80 continues to offer some alternatives, while Soi 90 to the south presents yet another option.In these locales, you'll encounter pocket-friendly drinks, an abundance of delectable food, and notably more amicable girls.When it comes to indulging in an oil massage at these Hua Hin nightlife establishments, it's generally expected that you'll disrobe and lie facedown.Any extras beyond the massage itself translate to additional charges for the girls, and they certainly possess a knack for persuading a gent once he's already in a vulnerable state.
