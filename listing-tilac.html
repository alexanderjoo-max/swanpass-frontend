About Tilac

The famous bar that appeared in The Hangover 2!Tilac Bar Bangkok is the big daddy of all bars on Soi Cowboy, boasting a modern and slick vibe. You'll find it right next to Deja Vu at the Asoke Road end of the street.

Fun fact: it's one of only two massive triple unit bars in the whole area!Back in the day, folks called it a gogo bar, but nowadays it's leaning more towards being a coyote bar.

Regardless, let's just call it a damn good bar! It used to be hailed as the top gogo joint in all of Thailand by the Stickman Bangkok website, but times have changed, my friend. Baccara and Crazy House are the new kings of the block in terms of popularity, leaving Tilac to battle it out with Shark Bar for the bronze medal.

Still, you can't beat Tilac Bar's size and the kickass decor that has stood the test of time. Plus, it got a major shoutout in the Bangkok Hangover movie! So, if you're doing a Soi Cowboy crawl and hitting up multiple bars, make sure Tilac Bar is on your radar.

It's a must-visit spot that won't disappoint!
