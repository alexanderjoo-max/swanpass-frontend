About Saigon Star

Saigon Star, tucked away on the 9th floor of the Saigon Star Hotel in District 3 of Ho Chi Minh City, may present a modest exterior, but inside, it harbors an excellent massage service. Upon arrival, you'll be met with a warm welcome from a manager who is fluent in English and puts your comfort first. You have the option to select your preferred masseuse via an iPad, with the manager on hand to inform you of who’s available and to offer suggestions if needed.

Enjoy the sauna with a complimentary beverage and fruit to prepare yourself for the full experience. The lobby is smoker-friendly. Having visited twice, the consistent quality of the services stands out. The staff is young, attractive, and there's a sense of leisureliness to your visit.

The array of services includes Thai massage, oil massage, catbath, half nuru massage, 69, and BJ, each with slight variations across the different packages.
