About Rock Bar

68 Pasteur is home to a well-known place called Rock Bar. It's a favored spot on the street. You'll find quite a few ladies working there, and they are generally good-looking. The staff, however, appears to be a bit older on average compared to other bars nearby. The bar has some poles for dancing, but it doesn't happen very often.

Despite this, customers really like the place, and it often gets crowded at night. The highlight of Rock Bar is the pool table. If a customer can beat one of the ladies working there, they get a free pint of beer.
