About Delta Spa Bali - Men's Health Spa

Delta Spa Bali: The Ultimate Men’s Lifestyle & Wellness Destination

Delta Spa Bali is more than just a spa; it is a premier lifestyle hub designed specifically for adult men's health and leisure. As part of a nationwide network with over 25 years of experience, this location offers an expansive range of facilities, including a gym, swimming pool, sauna, steam room, and a relaxation lounge. It serves as an ideal venue for business meetings or post-work socialization, complete with a spa bar and karaoke amenities.

Guests can upgrade their experience with exclusive options like the Sultan Suite, a 90m2 luxury space with a private sauna and giant whirlpool, or specialized treatments such as Shockwave Therapy for men's vitality. Services and entry packages generally range from 600k IDR to 3 Million IDR, depending on the room choice (Standard to Suite) and therapy duration.
